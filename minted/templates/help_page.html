Getting Started

Here are some basic steps to get started using minted:

  • Step 1: Create some spending categories by clicking on Categories.
  • Step 2: Now you can add some expenditures by clicking on the desired category and then clicking Add Expenditure.
  • Step 3: Your overall budget overview will show up on your homepage.
  • Step 4: If you want to see some more detailed charts, click on Analytics.

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Question 1: How many categories can I have?

    As many as you want!

  • Question 2: What are points?

    Points are awarded every day for simply logging in. You also get more points if you log in everyday! You can redeem points for rewards in the reward shop.

  • Question 3: How will minted keep me on track to meet my goals?

    You will get notifications if you are close to exceeding or exceed your budgets and you will get extra points for staying within your budgets.
